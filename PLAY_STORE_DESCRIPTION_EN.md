# ZzzZ - The Never-Ending Puzzle

**Free and ad-free!**
We don't collect user data.

If you love number logic games, you'll love this letter challenge. Instead of numbers, you'll merge the alphabet. Start with A, merge it with another A to get B, and keep climbing to Z... and way beyond! The game doesn't end at Z; discover combinations like AA, AB, and keep challenging your mind on a board that never stops.

---

## Why play ZzzZ - The Never-Ending Puzzle?

- **Infinite Gameplay:** No limits. How far can you go? Test your strategy and patience in an endless challenge.
- **Addictive Mechanics:** Swipe in any direction (Up, Down, Left, Right) to move all tiles. Matching letters merge and evolve instantly.
- **Premium Design:** Enjoy a clean, modern, minimalist interface with smooth "train-style" animations that respond to your touch.
- **Full Customization:** Choose from multiple vibrant color themes and toggle between Light Mode and Dark Mode to play comfortably anytime.
- **Difficulty Levels:** From relaxing to "Impossible". Adapt the challenge to your skill level.
- **Helper Tools:** Made a mistake? Use "Undo" to take back your last move.
- **Auto-Save:** Your game is saved instantly. Close the app and continue exactly where you left off without worries.
- **Offline:** Play wherever and whenever you want, no internet connection needed.

---

## How to Play

1. Swipe your finger across the screen to move all tiles.
2. When two tiles with the same letter touch, they merge into the next letter of the alphabet! (A+A = B, B+B = C...).
3. Keep the board clear by merging tiles as new ones appear.
4. Don't let the board fill up!
